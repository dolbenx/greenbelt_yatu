About Us

Building A Greener Future Together & Protecting It

To restore Zambia's heart beat by planting 1 million indigenous trees through:

  • Youth led action
  • Community ownership
  • Revival of our natural heritage

To see a Zambia where every community is sheltered by its own lush green shield of indigenous forests and every youth is a proud guardian of the land.

  • A Green Community
  • Indigenous forests
  • Guardian of the land

Zambia is heating up, and the rains are changing. We can’t stop the sun, but we can grow the shade. When you join Greenbelt Yatu, you aren't just a volunteer;

  • You are a Founding Guardian of a greener Zambia
  • You are a Founding Guardian of a wealthier Zambia
  • You are a Founding Guardian of a cooler Zambia
