Our History

Journey Was Started

Year 2020
Foundation of Greenbelt Yatu

Greenbelt Yatu was founded with a clear vision: to restore Zambia's natural heritage and empower the youth to take charge of our environmental future.

See Our Achievement
Year 2021
First 10,000 Trees Planted

We hit our first major milestone by successfully planting over 10,000 indigenous trees in the Copperbelt and Lusaka provinces.

See Our Achievement
Year 2022
Expansion to 5 Provinces

Our reach expanded across Zambia, establishing community-led reforestation hubs in five major provinces.

See Our Achievement
Year 2023
Youth Guardian Program Launch

We officially launched the Youth Guardian program, training over 500 young Zambians in sustainable forestry and conservation.

See Our Achievement
Year 2024
Half Million Trees Milestone

Greenbelt Yatu celebrated the planting of its 500,000th tree, bringing us halfway to our 1 million tree goal for Zambia.
